Speech Enhancement for Robust Keyword Spotting

This demo presents examples from our speech enhancement pipeline for robust keyword spotting (KWS).
Each example shows a noisy utterance that was misclassified by the keyword spotting model,
alongside the enhanced utterance produced by our trained SGMSE model.
The enhancement restores speech intelligibility and enables correct keyword recognition.
